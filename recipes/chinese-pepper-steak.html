Chinese Pepper Steak

Description:

Chinese Pepper Steak is a flavorful stir-fry featuring tender beef strips and crisp vegetables, typically served with a savory sauce. The dish is known for its balance of textures and bold flavors, making it a popular choice in Chinese-American cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef top sirloin steak
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch squares
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch squares
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
