Sweet and Sour Pork

Description:

Sweet and Sour Pork is a classic Chinese dish known for its vibrant combination of crispy pork pieces and a flavorful sauce that balances tangy and sweet notes. Often served with vegetables and rice, it's a favorite for its contrasting textures and bright, colorful presentation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork butt, cut into 1 inch cubes

Marinade:

  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Frying:

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • ½ cup cornstarch

Stir-Fried Vegetables:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 stalks celery, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 medium onion, cut into wedges
  • 1 pinch white sugar, or to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 ¼ cups water, divided
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup apple cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ½ teaspoon soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, undrained
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
